Legal Services

Let us be your legal department. We offer patent and trademark registration, notary, business registration, and a variety of other legal services like transcription and legal research.

Notary Services

We offer a variety of hands-on business registration services. Let our team of experts help you with business name requests and registration. We provide full-service formation and registration services like consultancy, preparation of articles of incorporation, legal document filing, and unique business license registration.

Legal Services

Your business’ legal needs don’t have to come with a hefty bill. Our team of legal outsourcing experts can help with a variety of services, including legal transcription, intake answering, appointments, research and legal collection. With a variety of outsourcing options on the market, we put you first. Your legal needs are our top priority.

 

Business Registrations

Our notary services team is ready to help with a variety of notary documents. We cover everything from affidavits to will preparation. We offer certified true copies of documents, estate planning, contracts and agreements, travel consent letters, power of attorney, release transfers, statutory declaration, and so much more.

Trademark & Patent Registrations

Let our trademark and patent support experts help you build your most valuable asset- your brand. From creating a startup to protecting your brand, our experts cater to your needs. We can assist you with licensing, registration, valuation, opposition, drafting, filing, consulting and defending against infringement.
